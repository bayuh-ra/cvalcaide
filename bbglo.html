The BabyGlo Management System is a full-stack web application built to streamline the operations of BabyGlo, a baby products distributor. The platform enables customers to register accounts, manage profiles, submit order requests, and track their complete order history in one intuitive interface.

On the business side, administrators and employees gain access to tools for inventory tracking, sales order processing, delivery scheduling, and financial monitoring. Features such as real-time stock visibility, automated low-stock alerts, and organized purchase order workflows significantly reduce manual work and improve operational accuracy.
