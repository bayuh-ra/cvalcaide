Quezt is an innovative website tailored for the Human-Computer Interaction Class, presenting a rich array of services to facilitate seamless interactions between users and educational institutions. As a central hub, Quezt connects users to various programs offered by affiliated universities, streamlining the search process for prospective students. Its extensive library hosts a wealth of resources including videos, articles, and study materials curated by each university, enhancing learning experiences. Moreover, Quezt serves as a gateway to opportunities such as scholarships, specialized trainings, and incubation programs, empowering individuals to pursue their academic and professional aspirations. With a vibrant community platform, Quezt fosters open communication between users and universities, fostering collaboration and engagement. From exploration to engagement, Quezt revolutionizes the interface between students and educational resources, making the journey towards knowledge acquisition and personal growth more accessible and enriching.
